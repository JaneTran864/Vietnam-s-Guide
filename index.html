Hà Nội

This section leans into foods that feel specifically northern and specifically local: phở and bún chả, but also cốm, chả cốm, and kem Tràng Tiền near the lake.

✨ Phở

Phở 10 Lý Quốc Sư

Classic no-fuss phở stop in Hoàn Kiếm when you want a dependable bowl right in the middle of the city.

What to try: phở bò tái nạm or a mixed beef bowl, lots of herbs and lime.

Address
10 Lý Quốc Sư, Hàng Trống, Hoàn Kiếm, Hà Nội, Vietnam
🗺️ Open in Google Maps 🔗 Official page
✨ Coffee

Egg Coffee & More

Central Old Quarter egg coffee stop with early opening hours and an easy pin to save for mornings.

What to try: hot egg coffee first; add an iced version if you want a lighter second round.

Address
39 Bát Sứ, Hàng Bồ, Hoàn Kiếm, Hà Nội, Vietnam
🗺️ Open in Google Maps 🔗 Facebook
✨ Bún chả

Bún Chả Hương Liên

Well-known bún chả address in Hai Bà Trưng that works as a practical lunch pin, made famous by the Obama–Bourdain visit.

What to try: full bún chả set with nem rán (fried spring rolls) for the “Obama combo”.

Address
24 Lê Văn Hưu, Phạm Đình Hổ, Hai Bà Trưng, Hà Nội, Vietnam
🗺️ Open in Google Maps 🔗 Info page
✨ Ice cream

Kem Tràng Tiền

Very old-school Hà Nội ice cream spot near the lake, perfect for a quick stop when you’re walking around Hoàn Kiếm.

What to try: que ice cream in cốm or vanilla flavour, plus one cone if you feel like queuing again.

Address
35 Tràng Tiền, Hoàn Kiếm, Hà Nội, Vietnam
🗺️ Open in Google Maps 🔗 Official site
✨ Cốm

Cốm Làng Vòng

Classic Hà Nội young green rice from Làng Vòng, best in autumn but still worth visiting off-season for bánh cốm and xôi cốm.

What to try: cốm gói trong lá sen if it’s in season; otherwise bánh cốm or xôi cốm.

Address
36 ngõ 63 Xuân Thủy, Dịch Vọng Hậu, Cầu Giấy, Hà Nội, Vietnam
🗺️ Open in Google Maps 🔗 Info page
✨ Chả cốm

Chả Cốm Hà Nội

Fried pork patties mixed with young green rice; crispy outside, soft and fragrant inside — very Hanoian and very snackable.

What to try: chả cốm chiên nóng, on its own or with rice; look for greenish rice bits on the surface.

Address
5 ngõ 1 Nghĩa Tân, Cầu Giấy, Hà Nội, Vietnam
🗺️ Open in Google Maps 🔗 Info page

Hồ Chí Minh City

For Sài Gòn, keep it bold, fast, and satisfying: cơm tấm and bánh mì that are easy to squeeze between café stops and walking days.

✨ Cơm tấm

Cơm Tấm Sà Bì Chưởng

A strong save for cơm tấm when you want something filling, fast, and very Sài Gòn.

What to try: full “sà bì chưởng” combo — sườn, bì, chả with plenty of mỡ hành and fish sauce.

Address
179 Trần Bình Trọng, Phường 3, Quận 5, Hồ Chí Minh City, Vietnam
🗺️ Open in Google Maps 🔗 Facebook
✨ Bánh xèo

Bánh Xèo Đinh Công Tráng

Southern-style crispy rice pancake with shrimp, pork, bean sprouts and herbs, wrapped in greens and dipped in fish sauce.

What to try: one bánh xèo to share plus extra herbs; wrap pieces in leafy greens before dipping.

Address
46A Đinh Công Tráng, Tân Định, Quận 1, Hồ Chí Minh City, Vietnam
🗺️ Open in Google Maps 🔗 More info
✨ Bún thịt nướng

Bún Thịt Nướng Vỉa Hè

Classic Saigon bowl of cold rice noodles with grilled pork, herbs and fish sauce, great for hot weather and light lunches.

What to try: one bowl with chả giò (fried spring roll) on top, mix everything with the fish sauce before eating.

Address
Search “Bún Thịt Nướng Nguyễn Trung Trực, Quận 1, Hồ Chí Minh City, Vietnam”
🗺️ Open in Google Maps 🔗 Food guide
✨ Bánh mì

Bánh Mì Huynh Hoa

One of the easiest bánh mì addresses to pin before a busy District 1 day, stacked with cold cuts and pâté.

What to try: mixed cold-cuts bánh mì; ideal to split because it’s packed and rich.

Address
26 Lê Thị Riêng, Phường Bến Thành, Quận 1, Hồ Chí Minh City, Vietnam
🗺️ Open in Google Maps 🔗 Facebook

Phú Quốc

For Phú Quốc, this guide stays practical: island noodles, seafood, and pins you can mix with snorkelling or beach days.

✨ Bún quậy

Bún Quậy Kiến Xây

Local-style noodle stop in Dương Đông; DIY dipping sauce and hot bowls are perfect after a swim.

What to try: one basic bowl with shrimp and squid plus at least one extra serving of the DIY sauce.

Address
28 Bạch Đằng, Phường Dương Đông, Phú Quốc, Kiên Giang, Vietnam
🗺️ Open in Google Maps 🔗 Facebook
✨ Hải sản nướng

Dương Đông Night Market Seafood

Night market grills with scallops, squid, prawns and more, all priced by weight and cooked to order.

What to try: grilled scallops with mỡ hành, mantis shrimp if you see it, and a plate of grilled blood cockles (chem chép nướng).

Address
Đường Võ Thị Sáu, Khu Phố 1, Dương Đông, Phú Quốc, Kiên Giang, Vietnam
🗺️ Open in Google Maps 🔗 How locals eat seafood
✨ Bún kèn

Bún Kèn Phú Quốc

A very local noodle dish with minced fish cooked in coconut milk, served with herbs and crunchy bits — uniquely island-style.

What to try: one bowl with full toppings; don’t skip the fresh herbs and pickles, they balance the richness.

Address
Search “Bún Kèn Út Lượm, Dương Đông, Phú Quốc, Kiên Giang, Vietnam”.
